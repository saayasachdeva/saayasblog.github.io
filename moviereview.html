Movie Reviews!

  1. A Haunting in Venice
    release date: September 15, 2023
    review: 5/5 stars!

    Inspired by Agatha Christie's novel Hallowe'en Party, this book is about a solving a murder that happens during a seance. There are different suspects each with a different motive. There wasn't as much haunting as I would have hoped for, but overall I really enjoyed this movie. I would recommend if you like murder mysteries!

  2. The Nun II
    release date: September 8, 2023
    review: 5/5 stars!

    The Nun II is a horror film set in 1956. Sister Irene and Sister Debra travel to Tarascon, France, to go up against the nun spirit Valak. I loved this movie and I personally think it was better than the first one. I would say to watch the first Nun movie before watching Nun II. I definitely recommend to watch if you like horror movies!

  3. A Man Called Otto
    release date: January 13, 2023
    review: 5/5 stars!

    Based off the book A Man Called Ove by Fredrick Backman, this movie was about a grumpy old man called Otto who befriends the family that moves in next door. I recommend this movie if you're looking for something heartwarming.

  4. Insidious: The Red Door
    release date: July 7, 2023
    review: 3/5 stars!

    Insidious: The Red Door is the 5th of the Insidious movies. It's labeled as a horror film, but there wasn't much horror thoughout the film. It was mostly toward the end. You don't need to watch the previous Insidious movies to watch this one.
